In this study, we focus on the southwest region of LLNL. Based on textural analysis of core and facies model interpretation, four depositional facies - debris flow (7%), flood plain (56%), bar/levee (19%), and channel (18%) - are distinguished within the study area (with proportions given in percent). Importantly, depositional facies have a geometric context in the description of subsurface facies models and, thus, form a scientifically defensible basis for building site-scale models of subsurface hydraulic heterogeneity. Seven realizations were produced, each with cell size 3 m, 10 m, 0.3 m and dimensions of 1203 m, 2010 m, 144.9 in the x, y, and z directions for a total of 38,930,283 cells. The lower z boundary is at 78.85 meters of elevation.  Realization seven is shown here.
