William J. Bosl

Mr. Bosl is a mathematician/programmer for the ParFlow project. His research interests include geosciences, geostatistics and conditional simulation for modeling subsurface flow, and scientific computing.

Mr. Bosl earned an M.S. from the University of Michigan (Atmospheric Physics) in 1984 and an M.S. from the University of Pittsburgh (Mathematics) in 1986. He joined Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory in 1992. He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in geophysics at Stanford University.
